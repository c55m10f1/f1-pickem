PRIVACY POLICY
How we handle your data in the F1 Pick'Em pool.
ABOUT F1 PICK'EM

F1 Pick'Em is a private Formula 1 prediction pool for a small group of friends. The app is operated by the pool commissioner.

WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

We collect the following information when you register:

  • Name and email address (for account identification)
  • Phone number (for optional SMS reminders)
  • Race predictions and picks you submit through the app
HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used solely to operate the F1 Pick'Em pool:

  • Display your picks and scores on the leaderboard
  • Send SMS reminders before race weekends when picks are due
  • Send post-race results notifications
SMS MESSAGING

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from F1 Pick'Em related to pick reminders and race results. Message frequency is typically 2–3 messages per race weekend. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact the commissioner through the app.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to pool participants and the commissioner.

DATA RETENTION

Your data is retained for the duration of the F1 season. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the commissioner.
