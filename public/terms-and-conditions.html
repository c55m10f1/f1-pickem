F1 Pick'Em SMS Reminders program terms.
DESCRIPTION

F1 Pick'Em is a private Formula 1 prediction pool. Participants receive SMS reminders before race weekends to submit their picks and notifications when results are available.

MESSAGE FREQUENCY

You will receive approximately 2–3 SMS messages per race weekend during the F1 season (typically March through December). Messages include pick deadline reminders and post-race result notifications.

MESSAGE & DATA RATES

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. We are not responsible for any charges from your carrier.

OPT-IN

By providing your phone number to the F1 Pick'Em commissioner, you consent to receive SMS messages related to the pool. Consent is not a condition of participation — you may participate without receiving SMS reminders.

OPT-OUT

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further texts will be sent.

HELP & SUPPORT

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact the commissioner through the app.

LIABILITY

F1 Pick'Em is a free, informal prediction game among friends. No money, prizes, or gambling is involved. The service is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind.

CHANGES

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued participation in the pool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
